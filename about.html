About Us

Objective

Monde strives to foster a strong and inclusive MLBB community by promoting a healthy gaming environment, developing skilled players, supporting esports growth, and empowering leaders to nurture every gamer's full potential.

Vision

Monde aims to be a well-known and strong organization that leads the MLBB community worldwide.

Mission

To protect and promote a healthy MLBB community, helping individuals achieve their full potential as skilled gamers. Provide a mentally friendly and motivating environment where players learn, and leaders facilitate growth, nurturing every gamer.
