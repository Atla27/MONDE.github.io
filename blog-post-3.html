Meta Analysis – Updates on the Latest Hero Buffs, Nerfs, and Tier Lists

By Jeremy March 13, 2024

Introduction

The Mobile Legends: Bang Bang meta is constantly evolving, with developers regularly introducing hero buffs, nerfs, and adjustments to keep the game balanced and exciting. In this blog post, we'll dive into the latest updates, analyze their impact on the current meta, and provide an updated tier list to help you dominate the battlefield.

Latest Hero Buffs

Several heroes have received significant buffs in the latest patch. Here are the key changes:

  • Gusion: Increased base damage on his first skill, making him a more viable pick in the early game.
  • Layla: Reduced mana cost on her ultimate, allowing her to spam abilities more frequently.
  • Chou: Improved crowd control duration on his second skill, making him even deadlier in team fights.

Latest Hero Nerfs

Some heroes have been toned down to maintain balance. Here are the notable nerfs:

  • Paquito: Reduced damage scaling on his ultimate, making him less oppressive in the late game.
  • Esmeralda: Decreased shield gain from her passive, reducing her survivability in prolonged fights.
  • Lancelot: Increased cooldown on his dash ability, limiting his mobility.

Updated Tier List

Based on the latest changes, here's the updated tier list for the current meta:

  • S-Tier: Gusion, Chou, Kagura
  • A-Tier: Layla, Paquito, Esmeralda
  • B-Tier: Lancelot, Granger, Selena
  • C-Tier: Hanabi, Miya, Zilong

Conclusion

The latest patch has shaken up the Mobile Legends meta, with some heroes rising to dominance and others falling out of favor. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive gamer, staying updated on these changes is crucial for success. Keep practicing, adapt to the meta, and climb the ranks!
