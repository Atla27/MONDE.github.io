MLBB Tournaments: The Rise of the Philippines in the M Series

By Eiderf | March 15, 2025

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang – A Global Phenomenon

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) has been a dominant force in the world of mobile gaming, captivating millions of players worldwide. Since its launch, it has evolved into more than just a mobile MOBA—it's become a platform for tournaments, community engagement, and thrilling in-game events. For fans and players alike, these events bring an incredible mix of excitement, rewards, and competition. In this blog post, we will dive deep into the various MLBB events that have helped keep the game's vibrant community alive.

The M Series World Championship

The M Series World Championship is the largest and most prestigious tournament in the MLBB esports scene. Held annually, it brings together the best teams from around the world to compete for the title of World Champion and a massive cash prize. Over the past six years, the M Series has grown exponentially, becoming a cornerstone of MLBB esports.

The Philippines' Journey in the M Series

The Philippines has been a key player in the M Series since its inception. In the first M Series World Championship (M1), the Philippines placed 9th-12th among 16 participants, earning $4,000 USD. While it was a disappointing result, it fueled the determination of Filipino players to improve and dominate in future tournaments.

A year later, in the M2 World Championship, the Philippines secured a slot among 12 participants. Two teams represented the country: Bren Esports and Smart Omega. While Smart Omega was eliminated by Todak Esports from Malaysia, Bren Esports rose to the occasion. They fought their way to the finals, where they faced the Burmese Ghouls from Myanmar in an intense best-of-seven series. The match was a nail-biter, with both teams giving their all. In the end, Bren Esports emerged victorious with a score of 4-3, claiming the M2 World Championship trophy and a $140,000 USD cash prize. This historic win marked the Philippines' first world championship title in MLBB.

Since then, the Philippines has remained a dominant force in the M Series, consistently producing world-class teams and players. To this day, the Philippines holds the title of the undefeated World Champion in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang.

Inspiring the Next Generation of Pro Players

If your dream is to become a pro player, let the journey of Bren Esports and other Filipino teams inspire you. Keep grinding, give your best in every game, and never lose sight of your goals. With hard work, dedication, and a positive attitude, you too can achieve greatness in the world of MLBB esports.
